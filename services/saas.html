SaaS & Custom Software Solutions in Sydney

K&G Marketing Media architects and builds custom SaaS platforms and software solutions for Australian businesses. From multi-tenant architecture and admin dashboards to subscription billing and API ecosystems — we ship software that your customers will pay for month after month.

Multi-Tenant Architecture

We build SaaS platforms with tenant-aware databases, isolated data environments, role-based access control, and elastic scaling. Each customer gets enterprise-grade security and performance without enterprise-grade infrastructure costs. Our architecture decisions are made for scale from day one.

Admin Dashboards & Analytics

Every SaaS product needs a powerful back office. We build custom admin panels with real-time analytics, user management, billing integration, feature flags, and operational tools that let your team manage thousands of accounts from a single pane of glass.

API-First Development

Modern SaaS products need to integrate with everything. We build RESTful and GraphQL APIs, webhook event systems, and third-party integrations that let your platform play nicely with the tools your customers already use. Every API is documented, versioned, and built for extensibility.
