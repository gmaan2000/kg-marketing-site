How We Work

How does K&G use AI for marketing?

We deploy custom AI systems that handle real marketing tasks — not gimmicks. Our voice AI agent answers business calls 24/7, qualifies leads, books appointments, and sends info packs automatically. Our automation workflows connect your CRM, email, billing, and social channels so data flows without manual entry. Every AI system we build is trained on your specific business data and integrates with the tools you already use.

What does working with K&G look like?

Every engagement starts with a discovery audit — we map your current marketing stack, identify bottlenecks, and build a strategic roadmap. From there, we execute in focused sprints: launching campaigns, building automations, and producing creative assets. You get monthly reports with real metrics — traffic, leads, conversions, and revenue attribution — not vanity numbers. Our clients typically see measurable results within the first 90 days.

Which Sydney businesses do you work with?

We serve businesses across Greater Sydney — from cafes, restaurants, and hospitality venues in Manly, Bondi, and Parramatta to professional services in North Sydney, the CBD, and Chatswood. We also work with tradies, health and fitness studios, and retail businesses in Western Sydney including Penrith, Blacktown, and Liverpool. Whether you are a sole trader or a multi-location franchise, we tailor our approach to your size and goals.
